About Me

I am currently studying IT and working through courses in web design and networking. I enjoy understanding how things work under the hood and then using that knowledge to solve everyday problems, whether that means fixing a device or building something new.

The experience taught me how to deal with pressure, real-world issues that aren’t in any manual, and communicate clearly with non-technical people. Eventually, I’d like to combine that background with my studies to move into remote-friendly tech roles.
